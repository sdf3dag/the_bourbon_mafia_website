The Bourbon Mafia's Giving Back

The Bourbon Mafia's #1 priority is to give back to our communities!



One of our main goals as a group, is to give back to our community. One way to do so, is to raise money for different organizations that some of our members have a personal connection with. Since our group organized in 2013, we have donated over $45,000 to various causes. The money raised has come in various forms, but mostly from bottle raffles.
